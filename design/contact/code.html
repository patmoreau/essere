The Heart Behind the Space

Elena Vance

With over fifteen years of dedicated practice and seven years of international teaching, Elena created The Elevated Sanctuary as a response to the chaotic rhythm of modern life. Her approach is rooted in the belief that wellness is not a destination, but a tactile breath—a continuous return to center.

Trained in Vinyasa, Yin, and specialized breathwork techniques, Elena blends anatomical precision with a poetic philosophy. Her classes are designed to be a meditative architectural experience, where the body moves like silk and the mind finds an expansive, luxury silence.
